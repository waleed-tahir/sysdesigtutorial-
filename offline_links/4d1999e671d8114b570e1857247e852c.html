Beyond relational limits

Legacy systems slow your AI roadmap. Move past rigid schemas and manual scaling to a platform built for continuous innovation.

Rigid schemas block fast iteration

Relational databases were built for predictable, static data. AI development is experimental and fast. When models evolve, legacy systems force complex schema changes, migrations, and downtime. Don’t pause innovation to update a table.

Master schema design

Scaling shouldn’t be a risk

Vertical scaling reaches its limits, and manual sharding adds operational burden for engineers. AI workloads demand systems that scale out automatically when demand spikes. MongoDB Atlas is a data platform that can handle the unpredictable nature of AI workloads.

Automate horizontal scaling

Stop patching your data stack

Stitching together separate databases for search, vectors, and transactions creates a fragmented architecture. Bolt-on engines and synchronization pipelines increase latency, complexity, and operational overhead. High-performance AI applications require a unified platform where data moves without friction.
